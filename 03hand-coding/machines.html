Top 3 CNC Brands

  1. Mazak

    Country: Japan
    Known for: High-quality CNC lathes, multi-tasking machines (like their Integrex line), and 5-axis machining centers.

  2. DMG MORI

    Country: Germany (DMG) + Japan (Mori Seiki)
    Known for: Precision high-end 5-axis machining centers, CNC lathes, and mill-turn machines.

  3. HAAS Automation

    Country: United States
    Known for: Cost-effective, reliable vertical and horizontal machining centers (VMCs & HMCs), CNC lathes, and rotary tables.
