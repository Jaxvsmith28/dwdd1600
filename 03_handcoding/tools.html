Tools

The Lathe

A lathe is a machine tool that rotates a workpiece about an axis to perform various operations such as cutting, sanding, knurling, drilling, or deformation. The main functions of a lathe are to remove material from the outside of a piece, bore out the inside, and drill holes along the length.

The Mill

A milling machine uses rotary cutters to remove material from a workpiece. Milling machines can perform various tasks such as cutting, shaping, drilling, and slotting. They are highly versatile and can be used to create complex shapes and surfaces.

The EDM (Electrical Discharge Machine)

EDM is a process where material is removed from a workpiece by electrical discharges (sparks). It is especially useful for machining hard materials or materials that are difficult to machine with conventional methods. EDM can be used to create complex shapes and fine details with high precision.

The Grinder

Grinders are used for grinding or polishing surfaces to achieve high precision and finish. There are various types of grinders, including bench grinders, surface grinders, and cylindrical grinders, each designed for specific tasks.
